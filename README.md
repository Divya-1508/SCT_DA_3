#  HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze employee attrition trends, workforce demographics, and key HR metrics. The dashboard helps identify factors influencing employee turnover and supports data-driven HR decision-making.

---

##  Project Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across departments and job roles.
- Measure overall employee attrition rate.
- Identify the impact of overtime on employee turnover.
- Monitor important HR KPIs through interactive visualizations.
- Present HR insights in a clean and user-friendly dashboard.

---

##  Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1. Employee Attrition by Department
Shows employee attrition across different departments to identify areas with the highest turnover.

### 2. Attrition by Job Role
Displays attrition across various job roles, helping identify positions with higher employee turnover.

### 3. Attrition by Overtime
Compares attrition between employees who work overtime and those who do not, highlighting the relationship between overtime and employee retention.

---

##  Key Insights

- Research & Development experienced the highest employee attrition.
- Certain job roles showed significantly higher turnover than others.
- Employees working overtime were more likely to leave the organization.
- The dashboard provides a quick overview of workforce health using HR KPIs.
